Curd rice

Thayir Sadam-A Traditional South Indian recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice (preferably soft or slightly overcooked rice)
  • 1 cup curd (plain yogurt) (fresh, not too sour)
  • Â¼ cup milk (optional, to keep it creamy)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the rice soft (use slightly more water than usual). Let it cool completely.
  2. Mash the rice lightly with the back of a spoon or your hand. It should be slightly mushy but not a paste.
  3. Add curd and milk to the cooled rice. Mix well.
  4. Add salt to taste and stir.
